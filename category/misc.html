Sat 16 November 2013

Thanks to Heeyeon Jeong, she designed a set of beautiful Earth Reader icons for iOS, Mac, Windows, Android, and a favicon. Fresh green sprouts and brown ground remind us the etymology of Earth Reader; earth doesn’t mean the planet on which we live, but the land surface. We actually tried to find an adversative to cloud.
